About Work Words

Gray Bike is a small software consultancy based in San Francisco, California.

We're a small collective of designers and developers helping our clients build and improve their software. All projects are led by our founder, Pavan Trikutam.

We have worked as a technical partner for organizations of all sizes. Usually this involves a combination of design, code, and lots of collaboration. We love being involved in a project from the design phase, but we're happy to jump in at any stage.
