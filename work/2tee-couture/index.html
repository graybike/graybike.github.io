OVERVIEW

2tee Couture is a women's fashion line based out of LA. They approached me looking to update their out-of-date e-commerce platform & website. I helped them refresh their design (and make it responsive), migrate their entire store to the Shopify platform, and customized the back end so they could gain more control over their store.

WHAT I DID

For this project, I provided all the services, from design to development. I built the website on top of the Shopify platform, and designed & implemented a new look & feel. I also built some advanced customizations for their store to meet their various business needs. Of course, the entire site was built to be responsive so it would look good across all devices.

TECHNOLOGIES
  • Shopify App SDK
  • Front End (HTML/CSS/JS)
  • Back End (Ruby/Sinatra)
