OVERVIEW

SALT Technology launched a new product called OneTouch which allows merchants to provide a simpler, unified, easier to use checkout interface for their customers on mobile devices. This allows customers to easily store & reuse their credit card information with different merchants across the web, enabled by just a few touches during checkout. They approached us to help with design & engineering for the mobile interface, as well as guidance on integrating it with the back end.

WHAT WE DID

We followed a full design & engineering process, from idea to wireframes to a fully functioning prototype. Coordinating with SALT's internal engineering & product folks throughout the entire project, we went through multiple iterations of wireframes and design. As we built the prototype, we paid close attention to UX & interaction design.

TECHNOLOGIES
  • Ruby / Sinatra
  • Front End (HTML/CSS/JS)
